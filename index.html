Why Cardano

High Degree of Decentralization The Cardano network is run by thousands of independent nodes worldwide, ensuring decentralization and resistance to censorship. Anyone can participate in the network by running a node.

Years of Secure Operation with Zero Downtime and Zero Hacks Since its mainnet launch, the Cardano network has maintained stable operation without any downtime or hacker attacks, demonstrating its exceptional security and stability.

Run Nodes on Inexpensive Raspberry Pi Devices The hardware requirements for Cardano nodes are relatively low, allowing users to run nodes using inexpensive Raspberry Pi devices, thereby lowering the participation barrier and encouraging more people to join.

High Scalability Cardano employs a layered architecture and the Ouroboros consensus protocol, offering high scalability that can handle a large number of transactions and support complex smart contracts.

Peer-Reviewed Cardano’s development process strictly follows a scientific approach with peer review, ensuring the robustness and reliability of every piece of code and protocol.

Formal Verification Cardano uses formal verification methods, applying mathematical proofs to ensure the correctness and security of smart contracts and protocols.

Loyal and Engaged Community Cardano has a passionate and loyal community, with members actively participating in development, promotion, and ecosystem building, providing a solid foundation for the project’s long-term growth.
